To Luisa,

It's no secret that at times I completely turn off my brain and just talk about random things albeit serious at times, and things that I hardly ever would talk with anyone else on this planet. And, of course, I always looked forward to hearing from a girl with a sweet tooth!

Humble, Honest, Caring, Smart, Silly & Funny

You are a chaser of your dream aspiring to be a great helping hand to others in need. I hope the future is bright for both of us. All my moments with you have been filled with relief, joy and happiness. Things might change for the better or worse. But deep down, I will always remember you. I wish you and the people in your life all the best and maybe a little challenge to grow. May your aspirations come true DREAMCATCHER!
